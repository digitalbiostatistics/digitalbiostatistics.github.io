Marcos Matabuena will be an invited speaker at the 2026 Diabetes Technology Meeting, taking place in Washington, D.C., from October 27–29.
At one of the world’s leading international conferences on diabetes technology, he will present innovative statistical and digital health methods developed by our research group using glucose data, and discuss their potential applications in diabetes research and clinical practice.
