Why Develop an Ambitious Scientific Agenda in the United Arab Emirates?

A natural question is why one would choose to develop such an ambitious scientific agenda in a country with a relatively recent research tradition and at a newly established university such as MBZUAI. It is also worth asking why more scientists should consider moving to this region. There are several important reasons.

1. A Society Open to New Ideas

Emirati society is widely known for its hospitality, generosity, and willingness to support new ideas that contribute to social and technological progress. The United Arab Emirates has made artificial intelligence a national strategic priority and is investing extensively in research, education, and innovation.

This strong institutional support creates a favorable environment for scientists who wish to explore ambitious ideas and develop projects with potentially transformative impact.

2. A Unique Scientific Environment

MBZUAI offers a distinctive scientific ecosystem that is particularly supportive of young researchers seeking to develop original, unconventional, and potentially disruptive lines of research.

In many well-established academic systems, limited resources, pressure to produce immediate results, and an increasing administrative burden can encourage incremental and low-risk research. At MBZUAI, by contrast, we are encouraged to formulate large-scale scientific questions and to work on problems with the potential to generate meaningful social transformation.

This philosophy is closely aligned with the nature of the institution itself. MBZUAI was established as a university entirely dedicated to artificial intelligence and aspires to become a place where some of the next scientific frontiers of the field are defined.

3. A Strategic Location for Attracting Global Talent

The United Arab Emirates occupies a privileged geographical position at the intersection of Europe, Asia, and Africa. From this region, a substantial proportion of the world’s population can be reached within only a few hours by air.

This connectivity facilitates the creation of international scientific networks and makes it possible to attract talent from China, India, Russia, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and many other regions. For a global discipline such as artificial intelligence, this location represents an exceptional scientific and strategic advantage.

4. A New Geopolitical and Technological Landscape

The world is undergoing a profound geopolitical transformation. The centers of scientific, economic, and technological influence are shifting, and new regions are assuming increasingly important roles.

Within this changing landscape, the Emirati ecosystem offers particularly favorable conditions for innovation and entrepreneurship. Its capacity to make decisions rapidly, reduce certain bureaucratic barriers, and support ambitious projects enables scientific ideas to be translated into concrete initiatives with greater agility.

5. A Truly International Community

The United Arab Emirates is one of the most internationally diverse societies in the world. Its universities and companies bring together people from widely different cultures, scientific traditions, and educational systems.

This diversity is not merely a social characteristic; it is a scientific advantage. Humanity’s greatest challenges cannot be solved by a single discipline, culture, or region. They require communities capable of integrating different perspectives, questioning established ideas, and collaborating across national and cultural boundaries.

For these reasons, the United Arab Emirates and MBZUAI offer a unique opportunity to build a new scientific culture: international, ambitious, interdisciplinary, and committed to addressing problems of major importance to society.
